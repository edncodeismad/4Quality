About Us

Our mission is to offer high quality products from Italy.

Not only delicious foods and drinks but also a variety of expertly crafted products from natural raw materials, most of them having organic labelling, even wild herbs and plants that can only be found on the Alps and at the foot of the Alps.

Wines, liqueurs, herbs, teas, essential oils, cheeses, chocolate products, fruits, bakery products, beauty products ...

All these come from a carefully selected group of partner producers who share our vison and values<./p>

All of them are small or smaller artisans, all of them share the same distrust in industrialized methods of dealing with the land and the products that the land can give.

All share our drive to excel in quality and pleasure in doing a good job.
